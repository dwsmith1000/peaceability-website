# PeaceAbility CMS - User Guide for Content Editors

This guide is for people who will be adding news, events, and resources to the website via the admin panel.

## Accessing the Admin Panel

1. Go to your website URL and add `/admin` at the end
   - Example: `https://peaceability.org/admin`
   - Or: `https://peaceability-website.pages.dev/admin`

2. Click "Login with GitHub"

3. You'll be asked to authorize. This is normal. Click "Authorize"

4. You're now logged in! You'll see the admin dashboard.

## Adding a News Item or Event

### Step 1: Go to the News Section

In the left sidebar, click **"News & Events"**

### Step 2: Create New Item

Click the green **"New News Item"** button (top right)

### Step 3: Fill in the Details

You'll see a form with three fields:

**Title** (e.g., "World Peace Summit 2024")
- Enter the headline of your news item
- Keep it clear and engaging

**Date** (e.g., April 5, 2024)
- Click the calendar icon
- Pick the date of the event or news
- It will show in the correct format

**Description** (e.g., "Join us for our annual...")
- Write 1-3 sentences about the news or event
- Be clear and concise
- You can include details about what, when, and where

### Step 4: Choose Language

Before saving, check which language you're writing in:
- Look at the top of the form (you'll see a language selector)
- **English** = English content
- **French** (Français) = French content

You'll need to create **both** versions if you want the news to appear in both languages.

### Step 5: Save

Click the **"Save"** button (top right)

You'll see a confirmation message. Your news item is now saved!

### Step 6: Publish

After saving, click **"Publish"** (if available) to make it live on the website.

The website will update within 1-2 minutes. Refresh your browser to see the new item in the News column.

---

## Adding a Resource

Same process as News & Events, but:

1. Click **"Resources"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"New Resource"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** (e.g., "Peace Building Toolkit")
   - **Description** (e.g., "A comprehensive guide to...")
   - **Link** (optional - e.g., "https://example.org/toolkit") - external website URL if there's more info

---

## Editing Existing Content

1. Go to News & Events or Resources
2. Find the item you want to edit in the list
3. Click on it
4. Make your changes
5. Click "Save" and then "Publish"

The website updates automatically within 1-2 minutes.

---

## Deleting Content

1. Go to News & Events or Resources
2. Find the item
3. Click on it
4. Look for a **"Delete"** button (usually bottom left or in a dropdown menu)
5. Confirm deletion

The item is removed from the website (within 1-2 minutes).

## Translation Tips

The website is bilingual (English & French). Here's how it works:

### For News & Events

When you create a news item in English and want it to appear in French too:

1. Create the item in English (see above)
2. Click the **language toggle** (top of form) to switch to **French**
3. Fill in the **French translation** of the title and description
4. Click "Save"

Now the news appears in both languages on the website.

### Tips for Good Translations

- Keep translations roughly the same length as the original
- Translate the meaning, not word-for-word
- Use clear, simple language both in English and French

If you're not comfortable translating, you can:
- Leave it in English only (it will only show in English)
- Or ask another team member to translate it

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Forgetting to Save**
- Always click "Save" before leaving a page
- Look for the confirmation message

❌ **Not Creating Both Languages**
- If you want French AND English, create it twice (once in each language)
- Using auto-translate won't work well — translate manually or ask a French speaker

❌ **Very Long Descriptions**
- The preview in each column is small
- Keep descriptions to 2-3 sentences maximum
- Longer descriptions get cut off

❌ **Using Special Characters**
- Stick to regular letters, numbers, and punctuation
- Avoid emojis (they may not display correctly)
- Accent marks (é, ç, etc.) are fine

✅ **Good Practice**
- Use clear, short titles
- Write descriptions in simple language
- Include a date for news items
- Always save before closing your browser
- Test on your phone to see how it looks

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't log in"

- Make sure you're using the `/admin` URL correctly
- Check that your GitHub account has access to the website repo
- Try logging out and back in
- Clear your browser cache and try again

### "The form won't save"

- Make sure all required fields are filled (look for red asterisks *)
- Check that you have internet connection
- Try clicking "Save" again
- If it still doesn't work, reload the page and try again

### "My changes don't appear on the website"

- Wait 2-3 minutes for the site to rebuild
- Refresh your browser (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R) to clear cache
- Check that you clicked "Save" and "Publish"

### "I see an error message"

- Read the error carefully — it tells you what went wrong
- Common issues:
  - "Title is required" = you forgot to enter a title
  - "Invalid date" = the date format is wrong
- Fix the issue and try saving again

## Tips for Great Content

### News & Events

✅ Good example:
- **Title**: "Global Peace Summit 2024"
- **Date**: April 15, 2024
- **Description**: "Join PeaceAbility and global leaders for our annual peace summit. Three days of workshops, discussions, and networking focused on disability rights and peace-building. Register now!"

❌ Poor example:
- **Title**: "Event"
- **Date**: (no date)
- **Description**: "There is an event happening" (too vague)

### Resources

✅ Good example:
- **Title**: "Inclusive Peace Building Toolkit"
- **Description**: "A comprehensive guide with practical tools for building peace in diverse communities. Includes templates, case studies, and best practices."
- **Link**: "https://peaceability.org/toolkit" (link to the actual resource)

❌ Poor example:
- **Title**: "Resource 1"
- **Description**: "Click here"
- **Link**: (missing)
